Consumer Bankruptcy Guide

If you are considering bankruptcy or currently in a case, this section is for you. No legal advice. No attorney referrals. Just data and tools from 4.9 million federal bankruptcy cases.

The data on this site comes from the Federal Judicial Center's Integrated Database, covering every bankruptcy case filed in the United States. We do not sell anything. We do not collect your personal information. We built these tools because the information should be free.

About This Data

Every statistic on this site comes from the Federal Judicial Center's Integrated Database (FJC IDB), which records every bankruptcy case filed in every federal bankruptcy court in the United States. Our dataset covers 4.9 million cases across 94 judicial districts from fiscal years 2008 through 2024.

We do not use surveys, estimates, or self-reported data. We do not sell anything. We do not collect your personal information beyond what you voluntarily submit through our contact form. The tools and analysis on this site are open-source and available on GitHub.
