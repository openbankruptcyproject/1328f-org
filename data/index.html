Open Data

Downloadable Datasets

All statistics published on 1328f.org are derived from public federal data. We provide these exports so journalists, researchers, and advocates can independently verify our findings.

Every file below contains aggregated, anonymized statistics only — no individual case numbers, no debtor names, no attorney names. District-level data and de-identified case attributes only.

Methodology & License

Data Sources

Data derived from the Federal Judicial Center Integrated Database, CourtListener RECAP archive, and PACER (Public Access to Court Electronic Records). Case-level records are aggregated to district level; individual identifiers are stripped before export.

Last updated: March 2026.

For details on how each metric is computed, see our Methodology page.
