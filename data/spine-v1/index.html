§1328(f) National Research Spine v1

v1.0.0FY2008-202492 districtsCC BY 4.0Published 2026-04-20

Nine aggregate datasets covering the national §1328(f) violation landscape across 92 federal bankruptcy districts and 17 fiscal years. Derived from the Federal Judicial Center's Integrated Database (Chapter 13 Pending File) with proper casekey deduplication.

Every row in every file is an aggregate. No individual case numbers, debtor names, or attorney names. Safe to republish, redistribute, and reuse under CC BY 4.0.

The estimator uses a 0.43 coefficient applied to the measured count of prior-filer Ch. 13 discharges. That coefficient is an assumption pending empirical validation. See the methodology note section 5 for the formula and section 7 for known limitations.
