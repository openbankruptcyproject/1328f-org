2026-03-17 Screening

Section 1328(f) Full De-Staling Complete

Completed de-staling of all flagged cases across every federal bankruptcy district. 391,951 prior filers received Chapter 13 discharges with zero eligibility verification. In a 7-district sample, 264 confirmed violations where debtors filed within the Section 1328(f) statutory bar window. Filing-date-to-filing-date methodology per In re Blendheim. Attorney breakdown: 6 attorneys account for 73% of violations. Full results published in the methodology report.
