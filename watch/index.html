2026-03-20 Rules Committee

Empirical Data Submitted to AO Rules Committee

Submission to the Administrative Office of the U.S. Courts in support of Suggestion 25-BK-N, the Connelly/Kahn proposed amendment to Federal Rule of Bankruptcy Procedure 4004. The submission includes empirical data from 4.9 million cases across 94 districts, an open-source screening tool, and a public GitHub repository. The AO Rules Committee Counsel requested the submission be forwarded for posting as a related suggestion on uscourts.gov.

2026-03-17 Screening

Section 1328(f) Full De-Staling Complete

Completed de-staling of all flagged cases across 94 federal bankruptcy districts. 391,951 prior filers received Chapter 13 discharges with zero eligibility verification. In a 7-district sample, 264 confirmed violations where debtors filed within the Section 1328(f) statutory bar window. Filing-date-to-filing-date methodology per In re Blendheim. Attorney breakdown: 6 attorneys account for 73% of violations. Full results published in the methodology report.
