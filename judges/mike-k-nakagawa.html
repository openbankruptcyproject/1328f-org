District Context

Judge Mike K. Nakagawa sits on the D. Nev. bankruptcy bench. Across this district, the Chapter 13 dismissal rate is 57.0%, near the national median. Of 47,516 Chapter 13 cases filed between 2008 and 2024, 16,963 resulted in a discharge. Dismissal rates vary widely across federal districts and depend on local practice, judicial patterns, and economic conditions.

Section 1328(f) of the Bankruptcy Code bars Chapter 13 discharge for debtors who received a prior discharge within specified time periods. Prior filers who were dismissed—rather than discharged—are not subject to this bar, but may face other restrictions under Section 109(g).
