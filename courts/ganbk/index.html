What is PACER?

PACER (Public Access to Court Electronic Records) provides electronic access to case and docket information for federal courts. A PACER account is required to view most documents. Registration is free at pacer.uscourts.gov, though per-page fees apply for document access (currently $0.10/page, capped at $3.00 per document).

CM/ECF (Case Management/Electronic Case Filing) is the court's electronic filing system used by attorneys and parties to file documents.
